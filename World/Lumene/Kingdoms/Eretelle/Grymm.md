Once a peasant family on the shores of Eretelle, now the longstanding royal family. Their son spoke about visions, and so the family garnered support from the whole village to help him. He bid him farewell and years later, after believing in him all this time did he come back. With the help from his family, Aldrich was able to invent lifechanging equipment. This meant that Grymm were no longer just a peasant family. The realm's power grew exponentially and that placed them as the rulers of Eretelle. Aldrich was the standing leader of Grymm until the end of [[The First Flame 0-443.FL|The First Flame]].
